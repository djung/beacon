Disposition Options

If you choose burial, you may have to pay fees at the cemetery of your choice. Such fees may include grave or mausoleum space, opening and closing costs of the grave vault or grave liner, head stone or markers, endowment care, etc. Please check with your cemetery to see what they will require.

Refrigeration in lieu of embalming, places the deceased in a lower controlled temperature space to forestall decomposition.
