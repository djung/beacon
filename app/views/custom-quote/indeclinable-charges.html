Indeclinable Charges

Basic Service Fee - $800

The fee for our basic services and overhead will be added to the total cost of the funeral arrangements you select. (This fee is already included in our charge for all packages, direct cremations, and immediate burials, forwarding and receiving remains). This price covers:

Our staff and facilities to respond to your initial request for service 24 hours a day - 7 days a week.

  • Arrangement conference with the family.
  • Coordination service plans with the cemetery, crematory and other parties involved.
  • Clerical and administrative assistance in the completion of various associated documents and forms.

The Basic Service fee also covers a proportionate share of overhead costs such as utilities, facilities and equipment maintenance, as well as governmental compliance.

Disposition Permit - $12

A disposition permit is required prior to burial, entombment or cremation of a body. The Local Registrar issues the disposition permit after the documentation of death worksheet has been filed. This permit must be given to the cemetery official or crematory operator where the final disposition occurs.
