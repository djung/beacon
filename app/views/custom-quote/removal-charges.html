Removal Charges

This section is designed to provide an approximate cost for initial removal. During the removal process our funeral home will bring the deceased into our care from the place of death. These prices are quoted under the assumption that services will be provided within a 25-mile radius of Santa Monica. If the location is outside 25 miles please add an additional $3 for each additional mile.

Standard Retrieval Fee - $395

The standard retrieval fee to move the deceased from their current location

Extra Attendant Fee - $75

If the initial transfer is from a non-medical facility, such as a private residence an additional attendant is needed and an additional charge of $75.00 will apply. An additional attendant may also be needed if the deceased is over 275lbs.
