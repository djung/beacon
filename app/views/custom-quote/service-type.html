Service Type

You may choose any number or combination of the following. Each option is priced for a period of 3 hours during a weekday, between the hours of 9:00am and 5:00pm.

A viewing is a private family gathering. It normally consists of only immediate family: Parents, spouse and childern. It is not formal and provides a very intimate setting, allowing for very personal goodbyes.

A visitation is open to the public and is less formal than a service. Typically, there are no speakers and guests arrive and leave on their own accord within the time alloted.

A service is a large formal public gathering. Typically, there is a speaker and prayers and eulogies are given. Personal thoughts and memories of individuals are shared.
