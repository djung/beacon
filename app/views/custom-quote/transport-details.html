Transport Details

If you are having a gathering outside our Malinow and Silverman Chapel and require to have the decedent present you may want to use the hearse/funeral coach. If you do not want to use the funeral coach we can provide a white 2014 Dodge Grand Caravan to meet your needs free of charge.

The hearse/funeral coach is $300 per three-hour block. Each additional hour thereafter will be an additional $100.

+

A funeral procession, usually in motor vehicles, is from a funeral home, church or other place of worship to the cemetery or crematory. The deceased is usually transported in a hearse, while family and friends follow in their vehicles. The family has the option not to have a formal procession. Instead funeral guest can meet at the cemetery or crematory on his or her own accord.

If there is a formal procession law requires funeral processions to use motor escorts. Company policy requires one escort per six vehicles, with a minimum of two escorts. Each escort is $200.
